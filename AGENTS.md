## Implementation Discipline

- Do not implement broad rewrites unless explicitly approved.
- Preserve working implementation where possible.
- Work in small, evidence-backed slices.
- Keep user-facing product language free of internal development-process terms.
- After implementation tasks, provide the evidence pack described in `docs/specs/core-workflow/Annex_13_Codex_Implementation_Evidence_Pack_Template.md`.
